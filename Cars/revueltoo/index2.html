Laamborghini REVUELTO

The Lamborghini Revuelto is a hybrid supercar powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine and three electric motors, delivering around 1,001 horsepower. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 2.5 seconds, with a top speed over 217 mph. The car features all-wheel drive, active aerodynamics, and rear-wheel steering for exceptional handling, ensuring a dynamic driving experience. Its design is sharp and aggressive, with a lightweight carbon fiber structure that enhances both performance and efficiency. Inside, the Revuelto offers a driver-focused cockpit with premium materials, advanced digital displays, and cutting-edge infotainment, combining Lamborghini's iconic performance with modern hybrid technology and luxury. This model represents a significant leap forward for Lamborghini, blending tradition with innovation.

Laamborghini HURACAN-EVO-SPYDER

The Lamborghini Huracán EVO Spyder is a convertible supercar powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine producing 631 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 3.1 seconds, with a top speed of 201 mph. The car features Lamborghini’s LDVI system, enhancing handling and performance. Its sleek design includes a lightweight soft-top roof that retracts in 17 seconds at speeds up to 31 mph. Inside, the cabin is luxurious, with an 8.4-inch touchscreen and Apple CarPlay integration. The Huracán EVO Spyder offers precise steering, rear-wheel steering, and adaptive suspension, delivering an agile and thrilling driving experience with the added joy of open-air driving.

Laamborghini HURACAN-STERRATO

The Lamborghini Huracán Sterrato is a groundbreaking supercar designed for off-road adventures, featuring a 5.2-liter V10 engine that delivers 602 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 3.4 seconds, combining Lamborghini's signature speed with rugged capability. The Sterrato comes equipped with a raised suspension, wider track, and all-terrain tires, making it adept at handling challenging terrains. Its design is distinct, with protective body cladding, roof rails, and reinforced underbody protection, enhancing its off-road readiness. Inside, the Sterrato offers a sporty yet luxurious cabin with advanced infotainment and specialized off-road driving modes. This model represents Lamborghini's bold foray into merging supercar performance with off-road versatility, delivering an exhilarating experience on both paved roads and rugged trails.

Laamborghini HURACAN-STO

The Lamborghini Huracán STO (Super Trofeo Omologata) is a track-focused supercar designed for extreme performance. Powered by a 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine, it produces 631 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.0 seconds. The STO features lightweight construction with extensive use of carbon fiber, reducing weight and enhancing agility. Its aerodynamics are optimized with a large rear wing, air ducts, and a hood scoop, providing exceptional downforce. Inside, the cabin is stripped down for racing, with carbon fiber seats, minimalistic controls, and advanced telemetry systems. The Huracán STO is designed for those seeking a pure, race-inspired driving experience, delivering Lamborghini's most intense road-legal performance.

Laamborghini HURACAN-TECNICA

The Lamborghini Huracán Tecnica is a versatile supercar that balances track performance with everyday usability. It features a 5.2-liter V10 engine producing 631 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, allowing it to sprint from 0 to 60 mph in 3.2 seconds. The Tecnica offers precise handling, thanks to rear-wheel drive, rear-wheel steering, and advanced aerodynamics, including a fixed rear wing for added downforce. Its design is sleek and aerodynamic, with a focus on both form and function. Inside, the Tecnica combines luxury with sportiness, featuring premium materials, a driver-centric cockpit, and advanced infotainment. The Huracán Tecnica is ideal for drivers who want a supercar that excels on the track while remaining practical for everyday use.

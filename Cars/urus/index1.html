Laamborghini URUS-PERFORMANTE

The Lamborghini Urus Performante is a high-performance SUV that features a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, producing 657 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.3 seconds, with a top speed of 190 mph. The Performante version stands out with a lighter chassis, improved aerodynamics, and carbon fiber body panels, enhancing both agility and speed. It also includes an advanced all-wheel-drive system, active torque vectoring, and rear-wheel steering, ensuring superior handling. The interior combines luxury and sportiness, with Alcantara upholstery, carbon fiber accents, and cutting-edge infotainment technology. The Urus Performante delivers a thrilling driving experience while maintaining the practicality of an SUV.

Laamborghini URUS-S

The Lamborghini Urus S is a refined version of the Urus SUV, blending luxury, performance, and versatility. It features a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that delivers 657 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds, with a top speed of 190 mph. The Urus S showcases a sophisticated design with updated front and rear bumpers, new wheel options, and a more luxurious interior featuring premium materials like leather, Alcantara, and carbon fiber. It also offers advanced technology, including a customizable digital cockpit, a top-tier infotainment system, and various driving modes for different terrains. The Urus S balances high performance with everyday practicality, making it a versatile option in the super SUV category.
